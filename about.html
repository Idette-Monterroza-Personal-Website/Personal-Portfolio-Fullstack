These are some of the following things I enjoy doing!

Drawing

Drawing has always been one of my favorite hobbies! I don't do it as much as I like, but when I start drawing again it's always an inspiring time. My drawing skills have improved but not enough where I can draw hands! I believe once you can draw hands, you can draw anything. Hands are always a challenge for artists!


Gaming

Gaming will always hold a special place in my heart. I've been gaming since elementary school and my favorite Nintendo console was the Gamecube and Gameboy. The games that were on those consoles were some of the best! In college I joined the eSports Club where I played Overwatch Competitively. It was a great time. Nowadays, most of my gaming is on my PC where I play games like Stardew Valley, Phasmophobia, and The Binding of Isaac.


Going out to eat

I love eating! The best feelings in life, besides sleeping is eating one of your favorite foods. There are so many flavors in the world. I have always been in love with chicken parmesan, ramen and my mom's enchiladas verdes!
